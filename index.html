What's Inside

Everything You Need to Know About the Weather

Weather View packs professional-grade meteorological data into a beautiful, easy-to-use interface.

Current Conditions

Real-time temperature, feels-like, humidity, pressure, visibility, and cloud cover — powered by WeatherAPI and OpenWeatherMap for continuously updated, accurate conditions.

Hourly Forecast

Scrollable hour-by-hour forecast with temperature, precipitation chance, wind speed, humidity, UV index, and cloud coverage.

7-Day Forecast

Full weekly outlook with daily high/low temperatures, precipitation probability, and detailed condition summaries.

Radar

Live weather radar powered by Leaflet and Mapbox with animated precipitation, cloud coverage, wind, pressure, and temperature layers, for real-time access to current weather conditions.

Wind Analysis

Detailed wind speed, direction, and gust data. Displays cardinal direction and visualizes wind patterns at your location. Smart controls automatically change their color and icon based on current conditions.

Air Quality & Pollen

Real-time AQI with PM2.5, PM10, ozone (O₃), nitrogen dioxide (NO₂), and other pollutant breakdowns with health guidance. Also tracks 7 types of pollen — Hazel, Alder, Birch, Oak, Grass, Mugwort, and Ragweed.

Tidal Conditions

High and low tide predictions for coastal locations, displayed in-line with weather data for complete situational awareness.

* Terms and Conditions apply.

Astronomical Data

Sunrise, sunset, moonrise, moonset times, moon phase, and hourly solar radiation (GHI, Diffuse, DNI, and GTI) data — calculated with WeatherAPI and SunCalc for precision astronomical accuracy.

GPS Location

Automatic location detection with manual search fallback. Always shows weather for exactly where you are.

Light & Dark Mode

Fully adaptive light and dark themes that follow your system preference or can be toggled manually for comfort in any environment.

Detailed Modals

Tap any weather section to expand a full modal with charts, extended data, and additional context for deeper analysis. Every modal window also includes a full opacity slider — slide it all the way to the right to make it completely transparent, and all the way to left for 100% opacity.

Cross-Platform

Built with Expo and React Native for a native experience on Android and iOS, with full web support as well.

Visual Design

Live Background Animations

A layered, weather-aware animation system that gives the app a living, breathing feel — all running smoothly at 60fps.

Four Gradient Layers

The background is composed of four independent animated LinearGradient layers stacked on top of each other, each cycling at different speeds (6s, 8s, and 10s loops) to create a smooth, non-repeating fluid effect.

Three Animated Wave Layers

Three wave panels sit above the gradient, each sweeping horizontally across the screen at different speeds (10s, 18s, and 20s) and vertical offsets, creating a gentle, rolling ocean-wave effect at the bottom of the screen.

Weather-Responsive Colors

The gradient palette shifts automatically based on current conditions — blues and teals for clear skies, deeper purples and grays for storms and overcast, warm ambers for sunrise and sunset conditions.

Light & Dark Adaptation

All animation colors adapt in real time when switching between light and dark mode. Light mode uses brighter, softer gradients; dark mode deepens the palette for a richer, more immersive atmosphere.

60fps Native Performance

Animations run on the native UI thread using React Native Reanimated's native driver, ensuring buttery-smooth 60fps performance without interfering with JavaScript logic or API calls.

User-Toggleable

Animations can be turned off in Settings for users who prefer a static background or want to conserve battery. Disabling animations instantly resets all layers to a clean, static state.

Travel Companion

A full suite of on-the-go tools built right into the weather app — so you're always prepared wherever you travel.

City Guide — Powered by Ticketmaster

Real events near you, fetched in real time from the Ticketmaster Discovery API.

Location-Based Search

Finds upcoming events within 100 miles of your current GPS location automatically.

Category Filters

Filter events by category — music, sports, arts, theatre, family, and more.

Event Images & Details

Each event card shows artwork, event name, date, venue, and a direct link to purchase tickets.

Smart Caching

Results are cached locally by location so the list loads instantly on repeat visits.

Local Attractions

Curated list of notable points of interest for your current city and country, generated based on your GPS location — museums, landmarks, parks, and more.

Local Business Search

Find restaurants, cafes, hotels, ATMs, and more near your current location with real-time distance in miles. Results sort by proximity so you always see what's closest first.

Tip Calculator

Enter your bill, choose 15%, 18%, or 20% (or a custom rate), set your party size, and instantly see the tip amount, total, and per-person split.

Scheduled Events

Your personal event planner with two views: a scrollable list and a full monthly calendar grid. Tap any date to add or view events. Days with events are highlighted on the calendar.
